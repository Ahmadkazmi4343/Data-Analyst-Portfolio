Pizza Sales Dashboards

The pizza sales analysis leveraged a comprehensive set of tools including Excel, Word, Power BI, and MySQL to enable data-driven decision making. MySQL served as the central database, storing transactional data, customer information, and inventory records in an efficient schema. Excel spreadsheets analyzed key metrics like sales figures, popular toppings, and delivery times through pivot tables and charts. Word documented processes, reports, and SOPs, facilitating collaboration. Power BI connected to MySQL and Excel data sources, generating interactive dashboards with advanced visualizations for sales performance, customer segmentation, revenue forecasting, and inventory management. Python scripts automated data extraction, transformation, and loading across the tools, ensuring real-time analysis. This integrated approach combined Excel's analysis capabilities, Word's documentation, Power BI's business intelligence features, and MySQL's robust data storage, providing comprehensive insights to optimize operations and drive growth strategies for the pizza business.
