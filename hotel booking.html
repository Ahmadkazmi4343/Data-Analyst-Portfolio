Hotel Booking Cancellation Analysis

The hotel booking analytics project aimed to develop an Excel dashboard for visualizing and analyzing key metrics like lead times, lengths of stay, occupancy rates, market segments, and cancellations. Visualizations included histograms for lead times, boxplots for stay lengths, line charts for occupancy trends, scatter plots for revenue correlations, pie charts for market segments, and bar charts for cancellations. The dashboard provided a comprehensive overview to guide resource planning, marketing, pricing, and customer service strategies in the hospitality industry.
